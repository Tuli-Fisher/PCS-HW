Welcome to PCS Burger Bar — where bold flavors, fresh ingredients, and hometown comfort come together in every bite. We started with a simple goal: to bring the flavors we grew up loving to the heart of New Jersey. Inspired by backyard cookouts, road-trip diners, and the unbeatable taste of a classic American burger, PCS Burger Bar is more than just a restaurant — it’s a place where food brings people together.

Our burgers are handcrafted daily using premium cuts of beef, seasoned just right, and grilled to juicy perfection. Each one is stacked with fresh toppings, melty cheese, and our signature house-made sauces — all served on warm, toasted buns. Whether you like your burger classic, spicy, cheesy, or fully loaded, we’ve got a favorite waiting for you.

But we’re more than just burgers. Our hand-cut fries are golden and crispy, tossed in bold seasonings, and perfect for sharing (or not!). Wash it all down with an ice-cold soda, or grab one of our rotating specials for something new.
