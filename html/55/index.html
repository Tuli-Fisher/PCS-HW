We began with a simple vision

to bring the rich flavors and wholesome freshness of our Kentucky roots to the heart of New Jersey. Inspired by the comfort food we grew up with—juicy burgers, crispy fries, and classic sodas—we set out to create a place that captures the warmth and satisfaction of a true hometown meal. Every bite at PCS Burger Bar reflects our passion for quality ingredients, southern charm, and the joy of sharing food with family and friends. From our kitchen to your table, we're proud to serve a taste of home, right here in your neighborhood.
